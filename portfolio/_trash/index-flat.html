Web UI & Application Programming

The #1 Boating Site, Rebuilt

With over a million pages of products, classifieds, help articles, discussion forums, and electronic catalogs, updating iboats.com for the mobile era was no simple undertaking. Over a 12 year period prior, many programmers and designers had built isolated sections of iboats' web eco-system, using disparete template systems, style sheets and coding techniques. While functional, the entirety of iboats.com lacked visual cohesion and technical unification.

Using Google's recommendation of responsive design as my guiding principle, I led the re-engineering project to create a modern web eco-system that would work on any device, all without adversely affecting the sales or SEO rankings of the leading internet marine business.

The end result was a cohesive, intuitive experience for all devices that increased SEO rankings, traffic and conversions across the board.

Key Project Highlights

  • All website templates converted to semantic HTML5, stripping layers of uneccessary complexity and code "bloat."
  • CSS re-engineered and consolidated in a central repository of over 90 style libraries organized by function, with style guides for junior developers and marketers.
  • Progressive enhancement delivered a functionaly complete experience on Desktop PCs, Tablets and Smartphones, while mainting compatability with legacy browsers.
  • 100+ JavaScript libraries ported to jQuery, delivering the latest interactive features for desktops and mobile devices.
  • SEO Micro-data and canonicalization were added to enhance existing SEO strategies.
  • Code minification, distributed CDNs, and image optimization were employed to ensure the fastest page loads, even during peak traffic periods.

Interactive Video Marketing

When Maverik wanted an interactive video marketing tool, they needed to go outside their usual advertising channels to find the right technological solution.

Working in conjunction with Maverik and their marketing partner, Catapult Impact, I developed the interactive video kiosk system to help promote Maverik's new loyalty card programs.

Key Project Highlights

  • The video kiosk system features a simple, minimalistic interface that allows consumers to interactively learn about customer loyalty card programs, smart phone apps, and current promotions.
  • The video playlist and banner ads are remotely administered using an encrypted Content Management System.
  • New videos, ads & updates are pushed out to all remote video kiosks on a synchronized schedule.
  • Project was completed on time and on budget, with details and polish that greatly exceeded client expectations.
  • Built solely upon Flowplayer, jQueryUI and a LAMP stack, no additional middle-ware is present to introduce unknown security risks.

Unrivaled Shopping And Selection

Boat propellers are a complicated product to sell. Before a purchasing decision can be made, the consumer must determine which few propellers will work for their boats, then make a final purchasing decision based on price, performance and availability.

Before the creation of iboats.com PropFinder, it was impossible for a consumer to be informed about all available options from all OEM and aftermarket manufaturers in an easy to understand format. Each propeller manufacturer would publish their own product catalog, but comparison shopping was a tedious and complicated process, beyond the expertise and patience of most consumers. With iboats.com PropFinder, this complicated and confusing shopping experience has been simplified to perfection.

The PropFinder is the industry-leading e-commerce site for boat propellers, with more than 30,000+ orders and 2.5+ million dollars in sales annually.

I built the entire proprietary software stack that powers the PropFinder, from the relational database design, to the Perl-powered dynamic HTML generation, to the CSS and Javascript front-end, as well as a full suite of administration tools that allow the product manager to keep it up to date.

Project Highlights

  • The only market-wide propeller equivalency shopping guide in existence, online or off.
  • Over 25,000 unique propellers for over 2,000 different engines back to 1950, covering all major makes and types.
  • Over 30,000+ in annual orders, 2.5+ million in sales with excellent margins.
  • Top-tier SEO rankings for head terms and long-tail terms. Example
  • Direct co-operation with propeller and engine manufacturers ensures the most accurate results for consumers.
  • Compatible with all desktops and mobile devices.

A Million Permutations Covered

Boat covers are a made-to-order product, unlike most products in the marine industry and most products sold on the internet.

With 40,000+ boat models from 600 boat manufacturers, cataloging all the possibilities from each cover manufacturer into an easy-to-use shopping experience is no easy task.

iboats.com CoverFinder is the world's #1 cover sales engine. With over 150,000 combinations of boats and matching cover patterns, no other cover website can match it in functionality or scope. And since all competing products are shown to consumers simultaneously, a purchasing decision can be made more efficiently, dramatically increasing conversions.

Additionally, each product sku is built dynamically at order-time, using consumer-specified boat stats, fabric and colors; this greatly reduces the number of products that need to be databased and updated.

I built the entire proprietary software stack that powers the CoverFinder, from the relational database design, to the Perl-powered dynamic HTML generation, to the CSS and Javascript front-end, as well as a full suite of administration tools that allow the product manager to keep it up to date.

Project Highlights

  • The only market-wide boat cover equivalency shopping guide in existence, online or off.
  • The most boat models covered, with more fabric and color options than any other retailer.
  • Over 25,000+ annual orders and $4 million in sales at up to 50% margin.
  • Top-tier SEO rankings for head terms and long-tail terms. Example
  • Direct co-operation with all major cover manufacturers, with special promotions and pricing favorability.
  • Compatible with all desktops and mobile devices.

Product Finder

ProductFinder is iboats proprietary e-commerce application platform. E-Commerce apps built upon ProductFinder have a huge ammount of inate features, including:

  • Integrated admin tools.
  • Dynamic templating and themeing.
  • Object-Oriented database abstraction model.
  • On-demand database and dynamic HTML caching.
  • Integrated SEO and sitemap logic.
  • Mandatory input validation and sanitation to protect against SQL injection and cross-site scripting attacks.
  • Dynamic product SKU creation for custom product packages.
  • Integrated product feed export for affiliate shopping engines.

Application Search Cache

A custom-built indexing search cache, similar to Apache Solr. Used to accelerate autosuggest search boxes and relieve database load during peak traffic periods.

Data Feed Automation Tools

Automatically processes, sanitizes and corrects complicated product application information feeds.
